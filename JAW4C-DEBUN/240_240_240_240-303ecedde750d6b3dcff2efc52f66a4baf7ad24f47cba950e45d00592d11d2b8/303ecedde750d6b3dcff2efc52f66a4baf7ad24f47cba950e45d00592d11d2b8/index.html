Frequently Asked Questions

  • Wi-Fi 7 is the newest Wi-Fi technology, built for the high-demand internet needs of today. It’s faster, has lower lag, and handles more devices than Wi-Fi 6 or Wi-Fi 6E. With features like 320MHz channels and Multi-Link Operation, Wi-Fi 7 keeps things running smoothly, even when the whole household is online.

  • Wi-Fi 7 can be up to 4.8 times faster than Wi-Fi 6, making it a great choice for things like 8K streaming and VR gaming. With these speeds, you’ll enjoy smoother experiences across all your devices, even if multiple people are online at once.

  • Nope! Wi-Fi 7 works with all your current Wi-Fi devices. While Wi-Fi 7-enabled devices will get the best speeds, everything you own can still enjoy a more stable network and faster connection on a Wi-Fi 7 mesh system.

  • Yes, definitely! Wi-Fi 7 boosts performance for video calls, streaming, gaming, and more by cutting down on lag and congestion. With a Wi-Fi 7 mesh system, you’ll enjoy a smoother, more responsive connection—even if your home has a lot of devices online.

  • Wi-Fi 7 mesh systems are made for whole-home coverage. They offer faster speeds over larger spaces, thanks to features like Multi-Link Operation and 6 GHz band support. This means strong Wi-Fi everywhere in your home, so you can say goodbye to dead zones!

  • Absolutely! Wi-Fi 7’s super-low latency and high speeds make gaming and streaming ultra-smooth—even in 4K or 8K. Say goodbye to buffering and hello to a seamless, immersive experience with minimal lag..

  • Wi-Fi 7 is built to handle more devices than previous generations. Our Wi-Fi 7 mesh systems can connect seamlessly with dozens of devices, making it perfect for homes full of IoT gadgets, phones, tablets, and more.

  • Upgrading to Wi-Fi 7 future-proofs your home. As more devices and services need faster Wi-Fi, Wi-Fi 7 makes sure you’re covered. An upgrade now means you’ll enjoy faster speeds, better performance, and reliable connections for years to come.
